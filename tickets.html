Tickets Page

There are many ways to experience this amazing event. If you are unable to join us on your couch for the entire festival, you are able to purchase an "A La Carte Day Pass" and choose your favorite lineup of the event.

For those of you planning on "vegging out" with us all weekend, the "Full Experience Pass" is for you. This includes a collectible bracelet commemorating the event.

For a truly one of a kind experience with your inner circle, purchase the "Ultimate Access Pass". This will include the full weekend of festival access for four, a pack of commemorative bracelets, 4 collectible posters with the full band lineups, and a brief "pre-performance" skype session with members of a band of your choosing. This package will help you and your friends create a lasting memory of the festival, long after the world goes back to normal!
